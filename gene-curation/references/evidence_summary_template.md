# ClinGen Evidence Summary — template

> **Guidelines followed:** ClinGen *Standardized Evidence Summary Text* **v7.1 (June 2026)**,
> with scoring/classification per ClinGen Gene Curation **SOP v10.1**. State both versions in
> the final summary (the approval sentence cites the SOP version).

Fill the brackets. Mix/match sections per the ClinGen Standardized Evidence Summary guide
(v7.1); not every element applies to every curation. Keep it clear for a non-expert reader.

---

**[GENE] — [Disease] — [mode of inheritance]**

*[GENE]* (aka [synonyms]) was first reported in relation to [MOI] [disease] in [year]
([Author] et al., PMID: [XXXXXXXX]). [1–2 sentence synopsis of gene function.] [1–2
sentence synopsis of the disease's clinical features, especially if not obvious from the name.]

**Lumping/Splitting.** Variants in *[GENE]* have been reported in individuals with the
following disease entities: [list entities + OMIM]. Per criteria outlined by the ClinGen
Lumping and Splitting Working Group, we found [no difference / a difference] in [molecular
mechanism AND/OR inheritance pattern AND/OR phenotypic variability]. Therefore these entities
have been [lumped into one entity / split], [disease] (OMIM:[XXX] / MONDO:[XXX]). [Note any
phenotype expansion or disease overlap/mimic considered.]

**Genetic evidence.** At least [N] unique variants have been reported ([variant types:
missense, nonsense, frameshift, canonical splice, deep-intronic, etc.]). Biallelic /
heterozygous variants have been reported in at least [N] individuals from at least [N]
families ([PMIDs]). [Note recurrent/founder variants; segregation in N families.] [If capped:]
More evidence is available in the literature, but the maximum score for genetic evidence
(12 points) has been reached.

**Mechanism.** The mechanism of disease is [loss of function / gain of function /
dominant-negative / unclear] ([PMIDs]). [Optional 1-sentence mechanistic synopsis.]
(Omit mechanism if the classification is Limited/Disputed/Refuted/No-known-relationship.)

**Experimental evidence.** This gene-disease relationship is supported by [expression /
protein-interaction / functional-alteration / model-system / rescue] data ([PMIDs]).
[Essential experimental findings, incl. any in-vivo rescue.] [If capped:] The maximum score
for experimental evidence (6 points) has been reached.

**Other relevant information.** [Phenotypic variability / reduced penetrance / genotype-
phenotype correlations / treatments / non-PMID sources documented here / variants evaluated
but not scored.]

**Summary.** In summary, there is [definitive/strong/moderate/limited/…] evidence to support
the relationship between *[GENE]* and [MOI] [disease]. [Definitive:] This has been repeatedly
demonstrated in both the research and clinical diagnostic settings, and has been upheld over
time. This classification was approved by the ClinGen [GCEP name] on [date] (SOP Version 10.1).
